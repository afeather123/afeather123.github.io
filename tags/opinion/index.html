Advice From a Novice Programmer to an Absolute Beginner

Jun 12, 2018 10 min.

A few weeks ago, I attended a talk from a Google employee who gave advice about working in the software industry, sharing his perspective on how to get a job and continue advancing your career, what the most important skills are to foster, and lots of other valuable advice. The talk really got me thinking and I’m really glad that I went. One piece of wisdom that he shared was that even when you are just starting out, there is always someone lower on the totem pole then you; the person who is in the place that you just were, running into the problems you just learned how to solve.
